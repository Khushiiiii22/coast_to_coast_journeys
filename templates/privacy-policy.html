Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information

At Coast to Coast Journeys, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information.

Last Updated: January 2026

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information to provide and improve our services:

  • Name, email address, phone number
  • Payment details (processed securely via Razorpay)
  • Travel details such as hotel bookings and preferences
  • Device information and browsing data for improving user experience

How We Use Your Information

Your information helps us provide better services:

  • To process bookings and payments
  • To communicate booking confirmations, vouchers, and support updates
  • To improve our services and user experience
  • To send promotional offers (only if you've opted in)

Data Protection

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties except trusted partners (hotels, payment gateways, APIs) strictly for service fulfillment.

Our trusted partners include:

  • Hotel booking partners and suppliers
  • Razorpay for secure payment processing
  • Cloud service providers for data storage

Security Measures

We take your security seriously:

  • All transactions are processed through secure, encrypted payment gateways
  • We use SSL/TLS encryption for all data transmission
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • PCI-DSS compliant payment processing

Cookies

We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience. Cookies help us:

  • Remember your preferences
  • Analyze website traffic and usage patterns
  • Provide personalized content and recommendations

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data (subject to legal requirements)
  • Opt-out of marketing communications

By using our website, you consent to our Privacy Policy.

If you have any questions or concerns about your privacy, please contact us.
