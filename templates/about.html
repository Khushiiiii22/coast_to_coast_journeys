About C2C Journeys

Your trusted partner for seamless travel experiences

Our Story

C2C Journeys is a customer-focused travel company dedicated to making travel simple, transparent, and reliable.

We believe travel is not just about destinations, but about trust, comfort, and peace of mind. Our platform offers flight, hotel, and visa booking services through trusted global partners, supported by secure payment systems and responsive customer support.

Accreditations & Registration

Doing business as Coast to Coast Journeys, we are an IATA-accredited travel agency.

  • IATA Numeric Code: 14031474 (Validate at checkacode.com)
  • Registered under IATAN
  • License No: JAM1010725364890 (Registered in India)

Founded on the belief that honest service and dedication create long-term value, Coast to Coast Journeys is committed to operating with integrity, responsibility, and a strong focus on customer satisfaction.
