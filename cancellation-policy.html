Cancellation Policy

Guidelines for cancelling your bookings

We understand that plans can change. Here's everything you need to know about cancelling your bookings with Coast to Coast Journeys.

Last Updated: January 2026

General Cancellation Guidelines

  • Cancellation charges depend on the hotel's individual cancellation policy
  • Some bookings may be non-refundable
  • Free cancellation (if applicable) must be done within the hotel's allowed timeframe
  • Cancellation policies are displayed at the time of booking

How to Cancel a Booking

Option 1: Online

Login to your booking dashboard and select the booking you want to cancel. Follow the cancellation process.

OR

Option 2: Contact Support

Reach out to our support team via email or phone with your booking reference number.

Info@coasttocoastjourneys.com

Cancellation confirmation will be shared via email. Please keep this for your records.

Cancellation Charges

Cancellation charges vary based on timing and booking type:

Cancellation Timing Typical Charges*
Within free cancellation window No Charges
7+ days before check-in 10-25% of booking value
3-7 days before check-in 25-50% of booking value
Less than 3 days before check-in 50-100% of booking value
Non-refundable bookings 100% (No Refund)

*Actual charges depend on the hotel's specific policy displayed at the time of booking.

Important Notes

  • No-shows: If you don't show up without cancelling, 100% of the booking amount may be charged
  • Modified bookings: Date changes may incur charges based on rate differences
  • Group bookings: Special cancellation policies may apply
  • Peak seasons: Stricter cancellation policies may apply during holidays and peak travel periods

Cancellation Due to Force Majeure

In cases of natural disasters, pandemics, government restrictions, or other force majeure events:

  • We will work with suppliers to provide the best possible resolution
  • Credit vouchers may be offered for future travel
  • Refunds will be processed as per supplier policies

Need Help with Cancellation?

Our support team is available to assist you Monday – Saturday, 10 AM – 7 PM.
