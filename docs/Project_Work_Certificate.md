## Project Overview

Coast to Coast Journeys is a comprehensive **B2C travel booking platform** that enables customers to search, compare, and book hotels, flights, and visa services. The platform integrates with multiple third-party APIs to provide real-time pricing and availability from thousands of hotels worldwide.
